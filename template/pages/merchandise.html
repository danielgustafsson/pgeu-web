Merchandise

We sell merchandise, such as shirts and plush elephants, exclusively at our PostgreSQL Europe arranged events.

We also have an online store with a small selection of merchandise for purchase.

Merchandise for Events

If you're organizing a booth or a complete event, we can send you merchandise material, especially:

  • Stickers (white, black, glowing)
  • Lanyards

They all are free of charge.

If you're organizing a larger event, we can provide more, such as elephants, mugs, umbrellas, though that won't be for free.

Keep in mind that they all are subject to availability, and that we might need some time to deliver them.

Contact us for more details.
