Donate

PostgreSQL Europe appreciates all donations from individuals and companies in, or outside of, Europe. Large or small, everything is appreciated. For those who wish to pay a bit more and get some more in return, we also have sponsorship opportunities for the events we organize.

What will my donation be used for?

All donations received by PostgreSQL Europe will be used to help promote PostgreSQL in and around Europe. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Arranging the PGConf.EU and FOSDEM PGDay European PostgreSQL conferences
  • Assisting and arranging local PostgreSQL events
  • Promotional merchandise (t-shirts, elephants, pins, flyers etc)

Donate now!

PostgreSQL Europe is a registered non-profit organisation in France. This may mean your donation is tax-deductible in your country.

Donating using Paypal

To donate to PostgreSQL Europe using Paypal, send money to paypal@postgresql.eu.

Donating using bank transfer

If you wish to make a donation using a bank transfer, please contact us at treasurer@postgresql.eu and we will provide you with the information required to make a transfer.
